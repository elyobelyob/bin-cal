How to subscribe

  • Apple Calendar (iPhone / Mac) File → New Calendar Subscription… and paste the URL. Or tap the URL on your iPhone and choose “Subscribe” when prompted.
  • Google Calendar Open Settings → Other calendars → From URL. Paste the URL and click “Add calendar”.
    Note: Google refreshes subscriptions roughly once a day.
  • Outlook (desktop) Go to the Calendar view → Add Calendar → From Internet… and paste the URL.
  • Outlook.com / Microsoft 365 Calendar → Add calendar → Subscribe from web. Paste the URL and click Import.
  • Fantastical / other apps Use Add Calendar Subscription (varies by app) and paste the URL.

How it stays up to date

Your calendar is refreshed automatically every night from your council’s website. You don’t need to do anything — as long as you’ve subscribed (not imported), your calendar app will pick up the latest dates automatically.

You can share this URL with neighbours at the same address — they’ll get the same calendar.
